Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How do I get started?

Getting started is easy! Simply click the "Get Started" button at the top of the page, create an account, and follow the guided setup process. You'll be up and running in minutes.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Log in to your account, navigate to the billing section, and select the plan that best suits your current needs. Changes take effect immediately.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start your trial.

How secure is my data?

We take security seriously. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security protocols and regularly undergo security audits to ensure your information remains protected.

What kind of support do you offer?

We provide email, chat, and phone support depending on your plan. Our support team is available during business hours, and Enterprise customers receive 24/7 priority support.
